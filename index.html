Why Attend CHAIC 2026

The Caribbean Health & AI Congress (CHAIC) is an international conference that brings together physicians, researchers, entrepreneurs, investors, and technology leaders to explore how artificial intelligence is transforming healthcare. This event serves as a global platform for innovation, collaboration, and knowledge sharing in the rapidly evolving field of AI-driven healthcare. Participants will gain valuable insights into emerging technologies, real-world medical applications of artificial intelligence, and the future of healthcare systems across the Caribbean and Latin America.

What You'll Experience

Inspiring keynote talks from global healthcare and AI leaders.
Real-world demonstrations of AI innovations in healthcare.
Expert discussions on the future of medical technology.
Networking with researchers, investors, and startups.
Collaboration across healthcare, technology & research.
Startup pitch competition spotlighting the next breakthrough.
